Our Process

Planning

First, our team will work with you to outline your targets. This can be any app, website, or other component you want to secure.

Identification

Discovery

At this stage, we comprehensively test your assets for vulnerabilities. This includes every category of the OWASP Top 10 and other prevalent vulnerability types.

Report

At the end of the test, we will deliver a report containing our findings. Included with each vulnerability is a recommended fix, in order to make patching the vulnerabilities simpler.

Resolving Vulnerabilities

At this stage, our team will work with you to apply patches for each vulnerability identified.

Confirmation

Finally, we will confirm that all vulnerabilities identified are patched and no longer exploitable.
